\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Concrete implementation of \mbox{\hyperlink{classStrategy}{Strategy}}. 

Acts as the Concrete\+Strategy participant in the \mbox{\hyperlink{classStrategy}{Strategy}} pattern. It us used to initiate the Defense of a given army.
